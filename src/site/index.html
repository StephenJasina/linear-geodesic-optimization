Outages:

  • Left click + drag to rotate
  • Right click + drag to pan
  • Scroll to zoom
  • Esc to reset zoom
  • S to save a screenshot
  • G to play/stop the animation
  • H to hide/show the GUI
  • + to add a new tab
  • - to remove the current tab
  • ← to move one tab to the left
  • → to move one tab to the right
